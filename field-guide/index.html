Privacy Policy for Verdant Ledger

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Verdant Ledger handles information when you use the app.

Overview

Verdant Ledger is designed to work without creating an account and without collecting personal data for advertising or analytics.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly through Verdant Ledger.

Verdant Ledger does not include:

  • Third-party advertising SDKs
  • Third-party analytics SDKs
  • In-app account registration
  • Cross-app tracking

Information Stored on Your Device

Verdant Ledger stores app data locally on your device to provide core functionality, including:

  • Tracking statuses for Pokemon, recipes, and materials
  • Favorites
  • Projects and project items
  • Profile and app settings
  • Recent searches and notes

This local data remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear your device data.

Tracking

Verdant Ledger does not track you across apps or websites and does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Because Verdant Ledger does not directly collect personal data, we do not share personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

Verdant Ledger does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the latest changes were made.

Contact

For privacy or support questions: https://github.com/macdoum1
