Privacy Policy for PLNK

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PLNK handles information when you use the app.

Overview

PLNK is designed to work without creating an account and without collecting personal data for analytics or advertising.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly through PLNK.

PLNK does not include:

  • Third-party advertising SDKs
  • Third-party analytics SDKs
  • In-app account registration
  • Cross-app tracking

Information Stored on Your Device

PLNK stores limited gameplay preferences locally on your device using Apple platform storage (UserDefaults), such as:

  • Mute setting
  • Haptics preference
  • High score
  • Local achievement-unlocked flags used by the game

This local data stays on your device unless you remove the app or clear device data.

Game Center

PLNK supports Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements.

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, gameplay records (for example, leaderboard scores and achievement progress) may be sent to Apple through Game Center APIs. This data is handled by Apple under Apple's terms and privacy policy.

PLNK does not receive your Game Center credentials.

Tracking

PLNK does not track you across apps or websites and does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Because PLNK does not directly collect personal data, we do not share personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

PLNK does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the latest changes were made.

Contact

For privacy or support questions: https://github.com/macdoum1
